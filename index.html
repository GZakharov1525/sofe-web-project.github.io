About Us

We believe that the world belongs to those who do not fear to discover it. Our world is vast and rich, but how little we know of it! Vagabond is an initiative project with the main aim of providing enthusiast travellers with reliable information about different conrners of our globe - the less travelled paths that speak volumes about the world. If you think you are ready to take on the challenge and become the next explorer of the 21st century, grab your compas, read about the adventures of other vagabond wanderers, see their video and photo stories, choose the destination you like, and go out there, make it happen!

And when you are back on familiar ground, share your story with us as well, participate in our polls, help your fellow travellers find the best spot on this planet and leave your digital eco-footprint on our map of the world!

Diamonds in the Rough

Destination What You're Missing Out On
Oahu, Hawaii Hike the Haiku Stairs: known as "The Stairway to Heaven" it is a steep trail with a wooden ladder spiked into the side of a cliff.
Island of Mindanao, Philippines Hinatuan River: a crystal clear blue saltwater river which has been nicknamed "Enchanted River" due to the fact that it flows from seemingly nowhere. Perfect for snorkelers and divers!
Poland On the west side of Poland there is the "Crooked Forest" with over 400 pine trees growing at a 90 degree bend to their roots, explore this bewildering phenomenon today!
Sichuan, China In the north part of the remote region of Sichuan you will find Jiuzhaigou Valley. It stretches for over 180'000 acres and is known for its Tibetan villages, multi-level waterfalls and colourful lakes with completely clear waters.
Rotorua, New Zealand Known for its geysers, thermal springs, and bubbling mud pool this city was nicknamed the "Sulphur City". Visitors can soak at their leisure in the hot springs or participate in the water sports held in the region's 17 lakes!
Lofoten Islands, Norway Home to the world's largest deepwater coral reef this island is an archipelago off the coast of Norway. It is also home to fantastic wildlife like the otters, moose and puffins as well as many others!
