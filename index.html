About Us

We believe that the world belongs to those who do not fear to discover it. Our world is vast and rich, but how little we know of it! Vagabond is an initiative project with the main aim of providing enthusiast travellers with reliable information about different conrners of our globe - the less travelled paths that speak volumes about the world. If you think you are ready to take on the challenge and become the next explorer of the 21st century, grab your compas, read about the adventures of other vagabond wanderers, see their video and photo stories, choose the destination you like, and go out there, make it happen!

And when you are back on familiar ground, share your story with us as well, participate in our polls, help your fellow travellers find the best spot on this planet and leave your digital eco-footprint on our map of the world!
